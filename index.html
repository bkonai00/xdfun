About Bikram

Hi, I'm Bikram Konai, the creator behind XDFUN. I blend gaming, deep-dive tech reviews, and vibrant daily vlogs into high-energy content built for an incredible community.

What started as a pure passion for digital worlds and technology has grown into XDFUN—a central hub where I share my adventures directly from my studio in West Bengal. Whether I'm testing the limits of new PC hardware, exploring AI innovations, dominating in games, or taking you behind the scenes with my daily vlogs and cooking experiments, my goal is always to entertain, inform, and connect. Join the XDFUN community as we explore the cutting edge of tech and gaming together.
