--- --- Privacy Policy - xdfun

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is non-negotiable.

At xdfun (accessible from https://xdfun.in/), we are committed to protecting the personal information of our creators and their supporters.

Data We Collect

  • Identity Data: Name and Email Address (required for account creation).
  • Transaction Data: Amounts, timestamps, and messages associated with tips.

How We Use Your Data

We use the information strictly to:

  • Facilitate the transfer of funds from supporter to creator.
  • Generate real-time alerts for your live stream.
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent transactions.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

Data Security & Sharing

We use industry-standard encryption (SSL) to protect your data in transit. We never sell your personal data to advertisers or third parties.

Data is only shared with payment gateways as necessary to complete a transaction.
